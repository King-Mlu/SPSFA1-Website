Declaration of the Member

With this affiliation, we the undersigned, on behalf of the above-mention club/associate
member,pledge our commitment to participate at Soweto Primary School-
Football Association (SPSFA), also referred here-to as the association, to the highest
standards in a professional manner and with the utmost integrity.

With that commitment, we further pledge to:
a) always comply with the Constitution, Regulations, Rules, Directives and decisions of the
association and ensure that these are also respected by our own members Officials and
Players;
b) recognise the judicial bodies of the association and SAFA Ekuthuleni as specified in the
association's Constitution;
c) always be prepared to play all official home matches in the specified/provided home ground;
d) organise or participate in friendly matches only with the prior written consent of SPSFA;
e) ensure that any member with a role in the commitee, will be present at every committe meeting
unless an approved reason is provided.
