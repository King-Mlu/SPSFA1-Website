About SPSFA

Professionalising primary school football and
empowering communities across Soweto.

The Impact of Professionalisation

For decades, grassroots football in schools has
been informal. The SPSFA brings a professional
structure to primary school football, ensuring that
talented children receive standardised training
from a young age.

By introducing regulated leagues, certified
coaching, and proper facilities access, we are
dramatically increasing the chances for our youth
to succeed not only in professional fooball but in
life through discipline and teamwork.

Our Mission

To provide a structured, competitive, and
developmental football environment for
primary school learners in Soweto. We strive
to empower coaches, build infrastructure,
and create pathways for young athletes.

Our Vision

To be the premier grassroots football
organisation in South Africa, recognised for
producing well-rounded student-atheletes
and elevating the standard of sports
administration in schools.
